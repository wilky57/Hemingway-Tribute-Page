Here's a timeline of his life:

  • Jul 21, 1899 - Born in Oak Park, Illinois

  • 1929 - A Farewell to Arms is published
  • 1953 - Wins Pulitzer Prize
  • Dec 10, 1954 - Wins Nobel Prize
  • Jul 2, 1961 - Suicide at his home in Idaho.
